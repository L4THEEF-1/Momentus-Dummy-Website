History, Mission, Values

Momentus was founded in 2024 by Adam Akil and Latheef, initially as a small gig for extra money. The brand quickly gained popularity for its beautiful, unique and extraordinary pictures. The brand also quickly rose to fame because of its cheap and fast service.

Momentus's mission and values revolve around self-expression, individuality, and pushing boundaries in picture taking to create outstanding pictures and portraits.



With many 5 star reviews, this brand continues to serve and be the best service for photography all around the world.
